InstaBaby

InstaBaby is a social media platform that was created for mothers and expected mothers. You can create groups, invite other members to the groups, create albums, and journals to share memories about pregnancy and special moments. You can also edit and delete groups, albums and journals.
Skills used:
HTML, CSS, jQuery, Angular, Express.js, Mongoose, Amazon S3

Click/Keydown Keys and Drums

Click/Keydown Keys and Drums is made with HTML, CSS and jQuery. Simply click or press the key to play! You can play chords or make your own beat by playing keys and drums at the same time!
Skills used:
HTML, CSS, Angular, jQuery
